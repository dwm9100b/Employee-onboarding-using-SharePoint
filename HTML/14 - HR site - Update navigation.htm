As you'll note, a Team site has its navigation as vertical on the left side. There is now an option that allows you to change this to top horizontal. So let's do this.

  1. In the left nav, click on home for the HR site
  2. Click on the top right Settings cog wheel
  3. Click on Change the look
  4. Click on Navigation
  5. Click on Horizontal
  6. Click on Mega Menu
  7. Click Save
  8. Refresh your HR site

Now let's update the nav with our new lists.

  1. On the right side of the new top nav, click on Edit
  2. Your edits will take place in the left side of the screen
  3. Under Home, click on the +
  4. Change the type to be a Label, name this: Onboarding
  5. Under Onboarding, press the + again
  6. Change the type to be a Label, name this: Get to Work
  7. Under Get to Work, click on the +
