Second site will be an Employee Team site.

  1. You should be in your SharePoint tab, not SharePoint admin
  2. In the far left, click on the circle with +, select Site
  3. Type: Team
  4. Name: Employees
  5. Privacy settings: Public
  6. Press Next
  7. Add members: Add whoever you want to have access or everyone
  8. Click on Finish
  9. Once created and redirected to the new site
  10. Click on Settings (cog in upper right)
  11. Click on Site Information
  12. Under Hub site association, select OnboardingHub
  13. Save
