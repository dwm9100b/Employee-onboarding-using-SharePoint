We will now jump over to SharePoint.

Open your SharePoint in a new browser tab.

First site will be our Hub.

  1. Navigate back to Microsoft365.com
  2. At the far top left, click on the App launcher (looks like a waffle)
  3. Find SharePoint and click on it
  4. At the top left in SharePoint, click on Create site
  5. Type: Communication
  6. Name: OnboardingHub
  7. Click on Finish
  8. Once created, go back to Microsoft 365 admin center
  9. Click on Show all in left nav
  10. Under Admin centers, click on SharePoint
  11. Go to Active Sites in the left nav
  12. Select the OnboardingHub site
  13. From top menu, pull down Hub and click Register as a Hub
  14. Give it a name: OnboardingHub
  15. Save
  16. Still in SharePoint Admin, click on Settings in the left nav
  17. Click on Home site
  18. Enter the URL of the OnboardingHub site (/sites/OnboardingHub)
  19. Save
  20. Go back to your SharePoint tab, keep the SharePoint admin tab available for later
