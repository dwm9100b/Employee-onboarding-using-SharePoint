Third site will also be a Team site for HR.

  1. In the far left, click on the circle with +, select Site
  2. Type: Team
  3. Name: HR
  4. Privacy settings: Private
  5. Press Next
  6. Add members: Anyone that needs full access
  7. Click on Finish
  8. Once created and redirected to the new site
  9. Click on Settings (cog in upper right)
  10. Click on Site Information
  11. Under Hub site association, select OnboardingHub
  12. Save
  13. Click on Settings again (cog in upper right)
  14. Click on Site permissions
  15. Towards the bottom of the panel, click on Advanced permissions
  16. Select, put a check mark, by HR Members
  17. Click on Grant permissions, upper left
  18. Under Invite people, enter SG HR and select
  19. Click on Show options, uncheck the Send an email
  20. Click Share
  21. Left nav, click on Home to get back to the site
