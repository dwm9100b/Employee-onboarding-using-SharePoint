Let's continue working on our first automation.
If you are not already there, go to Power Automate.

Navigate to Solutions in the left nav, click on your New Hire Onboarding solution.

Edit the flow: When a new employee onboarding input form is submitted.

In this section, we will be working on getting an email template and sending.

  1. After the Apply to each WFEmployeeOnboardingTasks step
  2. Add a new step
  3. Search for and select: Get Items (SharePoint)
  4. Rename this step: Get welcome email text from list
    1. Site: /sites/HR
    2. List: WFOnboardingEmailTemplates
    3. Click on Show advanced options
    4. Filter: EmailType eq 'Welcome' and Active eq 1
    5. Top Count: 1 (This will only return the first active welcome template, just in case)
  5. Now we are ready to send a welcome email
  6. Add a new step
  7. Search for and select: Send an email (V2)
  8. Rename this step: Send welcome email (V2)
    1. To: {PersonalEmail} in Dynamic content
    2. Subject: {SubjectLine} in Dynamic content
    3. After you add the SubjectLine, your send email will be wrapped by an Apply to each
    4. Rename the Apply to each: Apply to each welcome email record
    5. Re-expand your Send email step
    6. Expand to show advanced options
    7. CC: {HiringManager email} in Dynamic content
    8. Body add your entry, like:
      1. Hello {FirstName},
      2. FirstName would come from dynamic content
      3. Add a blank line
      4. Search for {Body - Body content of the email} in dynamic content, select this
      5. Couple new lines after body
      6. User name: {varUserName}@yourdomain.com
      7. New line
      8. Password: {varPassword}
      9. New line
      10. Add any additional content as desired
  9. This finishes our work on this first automation.
    Make sure you save and then go back to Automate home
